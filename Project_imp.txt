This website - is a user-friendly web platform designed to digitize and simplify the day-to-day operations of a housing society. It replaces manual methods like spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups with a single, centralized system for both residents and the management committee.


Core Problem We're Solving - We are eliminating the hassle and inefficiency of managing society tasks manually. This will improve transparency, save time for the management committee, and provide a convenient digital experience for all residents.


For Residents:

Online Maintenance Payments: 
	Securely pay society dues online via UPI, cards, etc., and get instant digital receipts.

Facility Booking: 
	Check the availability of the clubhouse or party hall on a calendar and book it online.

Digital Notice Board: 
	Stay updated with all society announcements and receive real-time pop-up alerts for important 	meetings.


For the Management Committee (Admin):

Centralized Dashboard: 
	Get a quick overview of payments collected, outstanding dues, and recent bookings.

Easy Financial Tracking: 
	Generate bills and track payment status for all flats automatically.

Simple Communication: 
	Post notices and important alerts for all residents with a few clicks.
