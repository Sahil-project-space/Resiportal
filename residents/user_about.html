About ResiPortal

ResiPortal is a digital housing society management system designed to replace manual paperwork, WhatsApp groups, and spreadsheet-based management with a modern, user-friendly web portal.

🔹 What is ResiPortal?

ResiPortal provides a centralized platform for both residents and the management committee to handle society operations easily. It ensures transparency, accuracy, and convenience across all society activities.

🔹 Key Features for Residents

  • Online maintenance payment.
  • Facility and clubhouse booking.
  • Digital notice board for all society announcements.
  • Lodge complaints and check admin replies easily.

🔹 Terms & Conditions

  • Residents must provide correct personal details during registration.
  • Payments made through the portal are non-refundable.
  • Booking facilities should follow society rules and timings.
  • Complaint section must be used responsibly.
  • Admin actions are monitored for security and transparency.

🔹 Purpose of This Portal

The goal of ResiPortal is to digitize society operations, reduce manual work, and enhance communication between residents and the management committee.
